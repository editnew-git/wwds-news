Worldwide Digital Signage has completed an update to resolve a display layout issue affecting the right-hand content area of a city-related digital screen. The improvement enhances how social media content is displayed and removes the need for a third-party widget in that portion of the layout.

The issue was identified through a customer notification and reviewed by the Worldwide Digital Signage team. After revising and testing the layout in the s.a.m platform, the updated screen presentation was visually approved and is now moving through final documentation and device-level confirmation.

The resolution included custom implementation work supported by AI-assisted development tools. The update was not limited to an automated adjustment; it involved direct coding, review, and testing to ensure the layout functioned properly and displayed as intended.

Worldwide Digital Signage will complete final closeout steps by capturing confirmation from the live device, following up with the original notifier, and documenting the resolution before closing the related support item.

> This update reflects the kind of practical, responsive support our team aims to provide. By replacing the dependency on an outside widget with a custom-coded solution, we were able to improve reliability and deliver a cleaner, more dependable screen layout for the customer.
>
> — Worldwide Digital Signage representative
