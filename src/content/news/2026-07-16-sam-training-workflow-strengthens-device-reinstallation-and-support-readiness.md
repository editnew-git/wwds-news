[Worldwide Digital Signage](https://wwds.co) is preparing a new public-facing training resource for the SAM platform, designed to help support teams and authorized users complete Sam stick operating system reinstall training with greater consistency and confidence.

The documentation is based on a recorded technical walkthrough that explains the reinstall workflow at a high level, including selecting the stable SAM operating system image, preparing a USB installer, booting the device from prepared media, connecting to a reliable network, and confirming that installation is complete. WWDS plans to convert the walkthrough into a Pressly knowledge-base article with screenshots so future trainees can follow the process more easily.

The resource is intended to reduce friction during support events, improve repeatability across reinstall scenarios, and give team members a clearer reference when assisting customers or field devices. It also reinforces the importance of coordinating device updates through the proper support process before and during any reinstall activity.

Once finalized, the documentation will support onboarding, technical training, and operational continuity for SAM-enabled signage deployments.
